Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a free professional invoice? +
Fill in your business name and client name, add your line items with description, quantity and price, optionally add a tax percentage, then click Download Invoice PDF. A clean, professional PDF is created and downloaded instantly — no signup needed.
Can I add my company logo? +
Yes. Click the logo upload area at the top and select a PNG or JPG file. Your logo will appear in the top-left corner of the generated PDF alongside the invoice number and date.
Is my invoice data stored anywhere? +
No. The invoice is generated entirely in your browser using jsPDF. No data is sent to any server. Close the tab and the data is gone — download your PDF before closing.
How many line items can I add? +
There is no hard limit. Click "+ Add Item" as many times as needed. For very long invoices with many items, the PDF will automatically accommodate them. Note: long descriptions are truncated at 44 characters in the PDF layout.
