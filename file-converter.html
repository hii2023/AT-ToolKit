Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a PDF to JPG for free? +
Upload your PDF using the dropzone above. The tool will detect it's a PDF and show JPG and PNG as output options. Select JPG, adjust quality, and click Convert. Each page is rendered as a separate JPG image at 2x resolution. Download them one by one or all as a ZIP file.
Can I convert multiple images to a single PDF? +
Yes. Upload your images — you can drag multiple files at once or use the "+ Add more images" button after the first upload. Select "PDF Document" as the output, choose your page size (Fit to image, A4, or Letter), and click Convert. All images are combined into a single PDF in the order uploaded.
How do I convert PNG to JPG online? +
Upload your PNG, select "JPG" from the Convert To dropdown, set your quality (85% is recommended for a good balance), and click Convert & Download. For multiple PNGs, they will be packaged into a ZIP file automatically.
Is there a file size limit? +
There is no enforced server-side limit since your files never leave your browser. In practice, very large PDFs (100+ MB) may take longer to process depending on your device. Most files up to 50 MB work smoothly.
Are my files private? +
Completely. All conversion runs inside your browser using JavaScript. Nothing is sent to any server. Your files are never stored. Once you close the tab, everything is gone.
