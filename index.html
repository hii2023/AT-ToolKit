Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a PDF to JPG for free? +
Open TempTool's File Converter, upload your PDF, select "JPG Images" from the Convert To dropdown, and click Convert. Each page is converted into a separate JPG image. Download pages individually or all at once as a ZIP file. Free, no signup required.
How do I convert images to a single PDF? +
Use the File Converter tool. Upload one or more images (JPG, PNG, WebP), select "PDF Document" as the output, choose a page size (Fit, A4, or Letter), and click Convert. All images are combined into a single PDF in the order you uploaded them.
How do I compress a PDF to a smaller file size? +
Use the Size Compressor tool. Upload your PDF, choose a compression level (Low for best quality, High for smallest size), and click Compress. TempTool re-renders every page at a lower resolution to reduce the file size — typically 40-70% smaller. Your file never leaves your browser.
How do I convert PNG to JPG online? +
Open the File Converter, upload your PNG file, select "JPG" from the Convert To menu, set your desired quality level, and click Convert & Download. You can also convert multiple PNGs at once — they download as a ZIP archive.
Are my files private — do they get uploaded to a server? +
No. All processing in TempTool happens entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. Your images, PDFs and documents are never sent to any server. Once you close the tab, nothing is retained. TempTool is completely private and offline-capable for file processing.
Is TempTool really free with no limits? +
Yes. TempTool is 100% free. There is no account, no subscription, no watermark and no file count limit. All 7 tools are available to everyone, forever, at no cost.
