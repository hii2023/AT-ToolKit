Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a QR code for my website? +
Paste your website URL (e.g., https://yoursite.com) into the input box above. Choose your preferred size and color, then click Generate. Download the PNG and use it on business cards, posters or anywhere you like.
Can I create a QR code for a phone number? +
Yes. Type tel:+1234567890 in the input box (replace with your number). When someone scans the QR code, their phone will prompt them to call that number directly.
What resolution is the downloaded QR code? +
Choose up to 600×600px from the Size dropdown. For print use (business cards, posters), select 600×600 — this gives clean output at most standard print sizes.
Do the QR codes expire? +
No. TempTool generates static QR codes — the data is encoded directly in the image. They never expire and work offline. The QR code stays valid as long as the URL or content it points to is still accessible.
